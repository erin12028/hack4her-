AI safety briefing · Amsterdam
Centrum is generally very safe for solo female travellers. Stick to well-lit streets after midnight.
Vondelpark and Westerpark are great daytime spots. Avoid Vondelpark alone after 22:00.
The Red Light District is tourist-heavy but can feel uncomfortable late at night solo — go with company.
Watch for pickpockets on trams near Centraal Station. Keep bags in front of you.
